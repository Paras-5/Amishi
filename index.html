About Us

We are team Amishi

Nestled just 30 kilometers before the enchanting town of Shimla, Amishi Guest House welcomes you with a prime location boasting breathtaking views and comfortable rooms. This idyllic retreat provides the perfect vantage point to witness the beauty of nature's snowfall embrace. With its serene surroundings, Amishi Guest House offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Guests can indulge in the cozy ambiance while savoring delicious meals conveniently available on-site. Whether you're seeking a peaceful getaway or an opportunity to experience the magic of snow-covered landscapes, Amishi Guest House promises a memorable stay amidst nature's embrace.

Our Apartments

Amishi Guest House and Apartments offer a serene retreat in the heart of Himachal Pradesh, nestled in the picturesque town of Khandaghat. Amishi Guest House welcomes guests with its warm hospitality, providing a comfortable and homely stay. The charming ambiance and well-appointed rooms make it an ideal choice for those seeking tranquility amidst nature. Just a short distance away, Amishi Apartments present a convenient option for rental purposes, catering to diverse accommodation needs. Surrounded by the breathtaking landscapes of Himachal Pradesh, both the guest house and apartments offer a delightful blend of modern amenities and the natural beauty of the region, ensuring a memorable stay.
